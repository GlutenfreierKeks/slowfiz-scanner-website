What Do the Results Mean?

Each result type has a different meaning and weight.

EVIDENCE WITHOUT DOUBT

A cheat mod was active while you played on slowfiz.net, or a cheat binary was found with injection-related strings. Ban without discussion.

INJECT — Cheat Binary

An EXE, JAR or DLL on the system contains cheat-specific content, or the filename matches a blacklist.

DELETED_CHEAT / DELETED_LOG

Cheat files or log files were found in the Recycle Bin. Actively deleting evidence shortly before a screenshare is itself a serious offense.

MAYBE FLAGS

Suspicious mod files or logs were found, but no direct proof of active use on slowfiz.net. Staff decides on a case-by-case basis.

CLEAN

No suspicious findings. No known cheat mod, no cheat binary, no deleted logs. No action needed.

slowfiz Logs — Connection Proof

Shows how many log files contain a connection to slowfiz.net. Confirms that you actually played on our server.
